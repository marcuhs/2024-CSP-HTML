Check out NewJeans

Hover over the images to learn more about this legendary KPOP group's discography!

NewJeans Debut EP

New Jeans

Released in August 2022, this debut extended play topped the South Korean Circle Album Chart and became the best-selling debut album by a K-pop female act, with over one million copies sold.

OMG Single Album

OMG

Released in January 2023, this single album was their second number one on the Circle Album Chart and sold over one million copies in South Korea. Both singles became viral sounds on social media.

Get Up EP

Get Up

Released in July 2023, this second EP sold over a million copies and reached the top 10 on charts in multiple countries. In the United States, it earned NewJeans their first entry and number one on the Billboard 200.

How Sweet Single

How Sweet

The How Sweet album by NewJeans, released in May 2024, features the vibrant and fresh double single tracks "How Sweet" and "Bubble Gum," showcasing the group's signature playful style and topping the South Korean charts. The release reinforces their distinct Y2K-inspired aesthetic amidst a tussle with their company.
